Findings

ToM is emergent at scale: Only the 7B model shows any ability to predict minister vetoes (6% accuracy). The 0.5B and 3B models show 0% accuracy, suggesting theory-of-mind requires a minimum model capacity threshold.

6% is near-random: With 5 ministers, random prediction would yield ~20% accuracy. The 7B model's 6% suggests it is actively making wrong predictions, not just guessing randomly.
